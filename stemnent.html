CEO & Founder

Today Gregory has many different certificates in Python, HTML5, CSS, Javascript and many more. Gregory is also a handy man and is certify in TV wall mount installation, Smart Home installation etc. Gregory Stemn is a family man and who put Faith first. He resides in West Fargo, ND with his Fiance, their three kids, his Niece and an Australian Shepard.
